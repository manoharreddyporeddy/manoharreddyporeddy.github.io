Natural numbers ( N )


Counting numbers are Natural numbers
	
	1, 2, 3, 4,...

        ---------------------------------------------->
        1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8 ...

        Smallest number = 1,          No Largest number

    
    Smallest / 1st  natural number  is 1
    Largest / last natural number   does not exist. There is no largest number (Infinity)


Natural number - 1 = next   number ( its Predecessor )
Natural number + 1 = before number ( its Successor   )

    Successor of 17     = 17 - 1 = 18
    Predecessor of 17   = 17 + 1 = 16

             <- x's Predecessor     x       x's Successor ->
        ---------------------------------------------->
                x - 1               x       x + 1

    Successor of 10,00,000     = 10,00,000 + 1 =  10,00,001
    Successor of 99,99,999     = 99,99,999 + 1 = 100,00,000

    Predecessor of 3 = 3 - 1 = 2
    Predecessor of 2 = 2 - 1 = 1
    Predecessor of 1 = 1 - 1 = 0
        Is there any natural number that has no predecessor?
            Answer: 1
                Predecessor of 1 is 0, but it is not natural number
                The number 1 has   no predecessor    in natural numbers

        Is there any natural number which has no successor?
            Answer: No
                There is no largest natural number
